How We Effectively Interact !

Academic Reach for Intellectual Aspirants(ARIA) is a volunteer-based non-government organisation. It aims at solving the problem of inequitable access to education. The Fellowship recruits college graduates and working professionals to serve as full-time teachers to underprivileged children.

The volunteers at Teach For India have to go through a rigorous five-week residential training period. Once the training is over, they are placed in different schools for a period of two years. You can join the over 1,100 volunteers and be a part of their initiative. Our Drives During Hard Time's

how does it works:

  1. We are an organisation whose primary moto is to activate the budding minds of our children.
  2. In times of lockdown when we have lost physical reach to our students, we have merged up with many volunteers with an aim to connect to every student and give them a personal guidance regarding their hardships in studies and their personal lives.
  3. We periodically schedule webinars, online meets with our students to make them feel connected to the organisation.
  4. We keep them motivated by knowing their personal interests, this has been possible because of the personal engrossment of volunteers and the benefits digital era.
  5. We have utilized the lockdown period for our students benifits, by constant positive enforcement.
  6. Unlike other individuals our students dont get bored, but they always have something or the other to do.
  7. Along with the academics, we keep a tab over their basic requirements of food, internet connection, medical health, etc

Fellow Impact

In addition to direct impact on our children, Academic Reach for Intellectual Aspirants(aria) long-term theory is to develop leaders who will continue fighting towards educational equity. The impact of the Fellowship on developing Fellows is measured through their growth on the three commitments of Personal Transformation, Collective Action, and the Commitment to Educational Equity.
